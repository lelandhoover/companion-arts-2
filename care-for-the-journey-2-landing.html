Care for the Journey: Volume 2, CD

Michael Stillwater and Gary Malkin

More acclaimed messages and music for inspiring and de-stressing healthcare professionals and family caregivers

$12.95


Care For the Journey, Volume 2 is Companion Arts' newest audio resource. Given the widespread success of the first volume, Volume 2 lives up to high expectations as it provides rich daily insights and practical wisdom in easily-assimilated, musically-enriched moments. Like its predecessor, Volume 2 is both a source of inspiration and a quick, easily-assessable remedy for the stress of an unusually hectic workday. Acclaimed healthcare speakers and other leaders provide spoken messages amidst a wonderful musical backdrop provided by Gary Malkin, a multiple Emmy Award winner. Speakers include: Angeles Arrien, PhD; Joan Borysenko, PhD; Ira Byock, MD; Jeremy Geffen, MD; Richard Moss, MD; Christiane Northrup, MD; Christina Puchalski, MD; Rachel Naomi Remen, MD; Jean Watson, PhD, RN; and Michael Stillwater. Care for the Journey: The Learning Edition is an exceptional gift for healthcare and healing professionals.
